Welcome to SmartRepair

Hello, and welcome to the State Repair service known as SmartRepair, Highway and City repair servicer for your local state coverage.

All repairs provisioned by the SmartRepair team are not out of pocket, and are funded by your local government by State Tax, or other contributions made by third-parties.

State Assistance (Insurance) Program

To learn more about your coverage as a citizen of your state, visit the top of the page for the SAP, or State Assistance Program. As you are automatically eligable for this coverage on your local assets provided that they reside in the local state.
